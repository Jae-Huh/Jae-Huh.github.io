Reflection on EDA

  • What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

    • EDA to me feels like it's always open and welcoming. One of the reasons why I decided to join the community :)

  • What are your impressions?

    • I really like the friendly atmosphere. I read about the Three Pillars, and love that everyone is encouraged to help each other become not just a great developer, but also a better person.

  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

    • I am quiet but I like people so I'm hoping I can help people in my cohort go through the course together. Also, in my previous job, I worked part-time so I'll have to push myself to work longer hours and make sure I am focused. One of the good things about coding is that time passes so fast!

  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

    • It's only been two days since I started Phase 0, so there's not much that I feel has changed but let's see how it changes as we go deeper into the course!

  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

    • I am very excited to keep studying at EDA. It does make me nervous as I worry that I might not be able to keep up with everyone. I guess hardwork is the solution for that :D
