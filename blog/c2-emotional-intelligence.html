Emotional Intelligence

Search Inside Yourself by Chade-Meng Tan - Chapter 1

  • What learnings did you take from the chapter?

    • It was interesting to find out that work performance is largely influenced by emotional intelligence. Everyone can learn to be emotionally intelligent, so anyone has potential to become an outstanding performer if they have the will.

    I also wasn't aware of the connection between mindfulness and emotional intelligence. Training attention can control how we manage our emotions which is the core basis for improving emotional intelligence.

  • How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

    • It's a very easy way to get into practising mindfulness meditation. I'm going to try it myself and see if I can stick to it long enough to make it a habit while increasing the duration over time.
