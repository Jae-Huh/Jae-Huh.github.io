Search Inside Yourself by Chade-Meng Tan - Chapter 7

  • Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

    • When I meet new people, I always tell myself to start off liking them until there is a reason not to. Because of this, I tend to be less defensive of myself and share personal stories with them more which I believe encourages others to talk about themselves as well.

  • Try empathic listening - how did it go?

    • I haven't got a chance to try empathic listening yet, but I like the idea of doing the loving kindness meditation in preparation for empathic listening. I'd have positive thoughts about the other person even before starting to talk with them, and that would be a good cornerstone for the listening practice.

  • How did you find the loving kindness meditation?

    • The loving kindness meditation left me with good vibes and it was a nice reminder of what makes the person the way that person is. Next time if there is a person that I find unpleasant, I'd like to try loving kindness meditation with that person's image to see whether it can help me understand his or her behaviour and how it affects my feelings towards the person afterwards.
