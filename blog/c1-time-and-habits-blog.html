Time and Habits

  • Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

    • I tried sensory meditation focusing on breathing and body parts, but unfortunately, I didn't make it a habbit in my daily life as I kept on forgetting to meditate. I didn't know that meditation could be done in the form of movement. In this article, an example of moving meditation was knitting. I crochet as a hobby, and I realised I was meditating all this time without even realising!

  • What did you find interesting?

    • I have heard a few people claiming the Pomodoro technique works really well. I haven't tried it myself, and I would like to try it through my study at EDA.

  • What worked for you?

    • I think meditation worked quite well for me. It makes me feel calm and relaxed.

  • Which will you experiment further with?

    • I might try making meditation a habit using an app. I would also like to develop growth mindset by starying small and through small acheivements.
